EXECUTIVE TEAM

Our nonprofit relies on the hard work of our team, working tirelessly to bring fresh content and big ideas to our organization.

Our team is comprised of two parts: a board and chapter officer teams of high school students, and an advisor team of college students.
Below is our executive team; chapter officers get their own designated section within their respective chapter page.

Siddharth Maruvada
Chief Executive Officer

Siddharth Maruvada is the NSSA Raleigh founder and a Park Scholar at North Carolina State University. He has been a part of NSSA since 2020 and enjoys teaching public speaking to students of all ages. In his free time, he likes running and biking.

Contact:
siddharth@nationalstudentspeakers.org

Diya Patel
Presidential Board Chairman

Diya Patel is a student at Thales Academy High School. She enjoys participating in DECA competitions and was a NC State Finalist in 2023. In her free time she enjoys her 3 Vs: Violin, Volleyball, and Voice.

Contact:
diya@nationalstudentspeakers.org
