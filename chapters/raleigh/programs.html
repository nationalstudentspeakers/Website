NSSA RALEIGH PROGRAMS

The programs of the NSSA Raleigh Chapter.

All videos, slides, and supplementary materials from these programs can be found on the the resources page.

Art Workshop & Competition

Raleigh's third workshop was done by Chieko Murasugi, an artist who is a curatorial team member at Basement Art Space. She creates paintings and other forms of art using various mixed media. This workshop featured art history, techniques, and sample work created by Ms. Chieko. It also featured a Q & A Session for participants to interact and ask questions about art. Following the workshop, an art competition was held for attendees of the workshop.

Speaking Up For Success Workshop

Raleigh's second workshop was done by Jezra Kaye, President of Speak Up For Success, Author, and Speaking Coach, about different public speaking styles, tips, and tricks over Zoom. It also featured a Q & A session for participants to interact and practice skills.

College Admissions Workshop

Raleigh's first workshop was done by Mike Steadman, Admissions and Recruitment Coordinator for NC State University, about both admissions and applications over Zoom. It also featured a Q & A session for participants to ask their most pressing questions directly.
